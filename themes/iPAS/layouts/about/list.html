Our Mission

Our mission is to help our clients grow through qualified and cost-effective remote specialized staffing, available round the clock, across all timezones. Through this, we help them become highly productive businesses delivering quality projects with the fastest turnaround times amongst their competitors. Choose iPAS for all your electrical and automation systems design and engineering needs and let us help your business grow and succeed.

iPAS also provides on-site construction administration services, on need basis, to ensure electrical manufacturers and contractors provide and install systems according to the approved design plans and specifications. At the end of each project, iPAS follows through to provide our clients with complete and accurate as-built drawings, manuals, and project technical documentation.

Our Values

Precision and Accuracy

In working out a solution to your system, iPAS walks with you from the conceptualization of the project through to thorough implementation of the solutions and beyond, to ensure long-term stability and efficiency of your system. Cognizant of the criticality of the task at hand, accuracy and precision lies at the very core of all our operations.

Quality

Quality and safety are key aspects that we consider in our solutions’ design, bearing in mind the need to implement cost effective but reliable systems for uninterrupted operation of our client’s industrial plants. Our comprehensive set of engineering design documentation and deliverables come in handy in ensuring high quality in our processing and delivery of solutions according to our customers’ requirements.

Integrated Approach

Cognizant of the fact that every project is unique in its own way, we take an integrated approach and work closely with project teams that may include mechanical and structural engineering partners, clients, architects, general contractors, and others, to work towards your project goals.

Adherence to Standards

Our designs and services are fully compliant with international engineering standards, and local regulations to ensure safety of your installation, team and ingenious quality through and through. With a team of highly specialized professionals with proven track of delivering a series of both greenfield and brownfield projects, we ensure that your team walks with us throughout then journey to allow for a seamless transfer of critical design knowledge that will be used even beyond the project in the running of your plant.

Professionalism

Where your ideas end, our reality begins! As professional electrical and automation systems design engineers, we help our clients solve challenges and turn their concepts into tangible entities by providing prerogative industrial design solutions. Always ready to address any comments or concerns, and staying one step ahead in anticipating bottlenecks and challenges, we provide the highest quality of work and communication every client deserves.

Flexibility

With our high flexibility in thought, execution and our operations, our aims and values guide us in the ethos of our work. We bring passion, innovation and expertise to complete our projects on schedule, on budget and following government specifications. Pursuing work practices built on reliability, impelled by excellence, and guided by respect, we are determined to stretch the boundaries of possibilities and give you our very best.
